Privacy Policy for Gift Card Tracker

Last updated: [Current Date]

Welcome to Gift Card Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Gift Card Tracker is designed to work locally on your device. We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Gift card details (card numbers, PINs, balances)
  • Transaction history
  • Brand information

Data Storage and Security

All your gift card information is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure CoreData framework. We do not transmit or store any of your gift card information on external servers.

Apple Wallet Integration

When you choose to add a gift card to Apple Wallet, the necessary information is processed through Apple's secure PassKit framework. This integration follows Apple's privacy and security guidelines.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are handled locally on your device to remind you about your gift card balances. No notification data is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any of your information with third parties. All data remains on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual gift cards within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
